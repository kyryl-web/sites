Frequently asked questions

We will answer any of your questions

How long does it take to make an order?

Order completion time depends on three factors: painting complexity, writing style and size. The average lead time is 7 days without taking into account the delivery time. In some cases, the customer can reduce the time to 5 days.

Note: If you decide to change something in the picture, make adjustments - this may take additional time, which is not taken into account in the specified time frame.

Can I see the painting before I get it?

Yes. When ready, we will send you a digital photo of your picture by e-mail, so that you can, if necessary, make your adjustments and wishes, if any. You can also see the picture "live" in our salon. Inform your manager.

What format should be photography

We work with all known image formats! Through the site you can download JPEG, TIFF, PNG size up to 50 megabytes. You must have copyright or permission to use the image. If you have a different image format (RAW, PDF), you can use the FTP loader. If you want to make photo from paper photography (not digital), send it to us, we will scan, process and return photo together with your order!

Can I give special instructions to the artist

When you place your order on the site, you can add your comments in a special text box. You can also: combine objects from different photo to one or delete objects from photo, you can change the background, change clothes, outfits, make a color picture with black and white photo, and also order a picture with damaged or old photo.
